Presentation

JDiffPlugin integrates JDiff into jEdit. It features:

  • Diff overview
  • Synchronized scrolling

Usage

  1. Split your view in two
  2. Edit the buffers you want to compare.
  3. Activate JDiff (Plugins -> JDiff -> Dual Diff)

Note: if your view is not split in two, JDiff will unsplit the view and split it in two.

Options

You can configure whether JDiff should ignore case (default) or not.

You can also configure the color of:

  • Changed lines
  • Deleted lines
  • Inserted lines
